Detailed Description

A recipe to combine multiple recipes into a single 'expression' recipe, which should be considered a single entity for cost-modeling and transforms.

The recipe needs to be 'decomposed', i.e. replaced by its individual expression recipes, before execute. The individual expression recipes are completely disconnected from the def-use graph of other recipes not part of the expression. Def-use edges between pairs of expression recipes remain intact, whereas every edge between an expression recipe and a recipe outside the expression is elevated to connect the non-expression recipe with the VPExpressionRecipe itself.
